About

You can't learn everything. You can't keep all the fully developed concepts in your head. What's more, you shouldn't. When we scale down an already fundamental concept, we make room for new, bigger ideas.

We are programmers. Programmers, in their hearts, are architects. The first thing they want to do when they arrive at a place is to level it out and build something new and magnificent. We are not happy with the gradual termination: tinkering, repairing or creating a flower garden
